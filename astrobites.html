Astrobites

I joined the Astrobites Collaboration in 2022. Astrobites is a graduate student collaboration, and our goal is to write Bite-sized summaries of recent research for the public. We also go beyond astro-ph, covering important issues to Astronomy and Astrophysics. Here is some of my work, covering recent research, more general guides covering broad topics in astronomy, conference coverage of the APS April or AAS meetings and beyond.
